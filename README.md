<h2><strong>FCAI-Battle: The Ultimate Code War</strong></h2>
<p>Forget late-night debugging and stressful midterms&mdash;the rivalry at the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence has officially reached a breaking point. It’s time to settle the score where it matters most: <strong>right in front of the Khazog.</strong>
</p>
<p>In <strong>FCAI-Battle</strong>, you step into the shoes of either <strong>Hager</strong> or <strong>Mariam</strong> in a high-stakes, turn-based projectile showdown. Forget water balloons or bones; in this faculty, we settle our differences by launching high-end <strong>laptops</strong> at each other.
</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3><strong>Game Modes</strong></h3>
<ul><li><strong>1 Player vs. Computer:</strong> Test your skills against a ruthless AI. Choose your champion&mdash;Hager or Mariam&mdash;and fight through <strong>three progressive levels</strong> featuring different times of day:
<ul><li><strong>Morning Rush:</strong> Crisp lighting as the day begins.
</li><li><strong>Midday Heat:</strong> High stakes under the blazing sun.
</li><li><strong>Night Shift:</strong> The classic "crunch time" aesthetic where the only light comes from your screens.
</li></ul>
</li><li><strong>2 Players (Local Device):</strong> Settle the beef with a friend right next to you on the same screen.
</li><li><strong>2 Players (Local Network):</strong> Connect over the faculty Wi-Fi (if it's feeling generous) and battle from different corners of the building.
</li></ul>
<h3><strong>Key Features</strong></h3>
<ul><li><strong>Iconic Scenery:</strong> Every battle takes place at the heart of the campus, featuring the legendary <strong>Khazog</strong> monument as your backdrop.
</li><li><strong>Tactical Projectiles:</strong> Master the physics of the "Laptop Toss." It’s not just about strength; it’s about the trajectory.
</li><li><strong>Character Selection:</strong> Play as Hager or Mariam, each bringing their own vibe to the battlefield.
</li></ul>
<blockquote><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Watch the wind meter! A sudden gust can turn your top-tier MacBook into a flying paperweight that misses the target completely.
</blockquote>
